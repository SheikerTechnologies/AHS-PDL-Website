## Building Communities, Not Just Buildings

At AHS Properties, we believe that true development extends beyond bricks and mortar. Our new Community Outreach & Green Initiative program reflects our deep commitment to the communities we serve and the environment we all share.

### The Three Pillars of Our Program

#### 1. Green Development

We are implementing sustainable practices across all our projects:

- **Tree Plantation Drives**: 500+ trees will be planted across our developments this year
- **Energy-Efficient Design**: All new projects will incorporate passive cooling, natural ventilation, and energy-efficient lighting
- **Water Conservation**: Rainwater harvesting systems in all AHS developments
- **Waste Management**: Segregated waste collection and composting facilities

#### 2. Community Support

We are investing in the well-being of local communities:

- **Educational Scholarships**: Merit-based scholarships for students in Jolshiri Abashon and surrounding areas
- **Healthcare Camps**: Free health check-up camps organised quarterly
- **Skills Training**: Vocational training programs for local youth
- **Mosque & Community Centre Support**: Maintenance and development of community facilities

#### 3. Environmental Awareness

We are committed to spreading environmental consciousness:

- **School Programs**: Environmental awareness workshops in local schools
- **Clean-Up Drives**: Monthly clean-up initiatives in and around our developments
- **Green Building Certification**: Pursuing international green building certifications for our flagship projects

### Join Us

We invite our residents, partners, and the wider community to join us in these initiatives. Together, we can build a greener, more sustainable Bangladesh.

> *"At AHS Properties, we measure our success not just by the buildings we create, but by the positive impact we leave on communities and the environment."* — Md. Sohanur Rahman Sohan
