## Developing Your Land the Right Way

Land development in Bangladesh offers tremendous opportunities, but it also comes with unique challenges. Whether you own a plot in Jolshiri Abashon, Purbachal, or any other growing area, these five tips will help you navigate the process successfully.

### 1. Verify Your Land Title Thoroughly

Before any development begins, ensure your land title is absolutely clear. Work with a reputable legal professional to conduct a thorough title search at the Sub-Registry Office. Verify:
- Original ownership chain
- Encumbrances or mortgages
- Tax receipts and holding numbers
- Mutation status with the relevant Union Parishad or City Corporation

### 2. Understand Zoning and Building Regulations

Each area in Bangladesh has specific zoning regulations that dictate what can be built. RAJUK-approved projects like Jolshiri Abashon have clearly defined building rules regarding:
- Maximum building height
- Setback requirements
- Floor Area Ratio (FAR)
- Land use designations

Ignoring these can result in costly legal complications.

### 3. Partner with an Experienced Developer

You don't have to go it alone. Partnering with an established developer like AHS Properties offers several advantages:

- **Expertise**: Years of experience navigating regulatory requirements
- **Capital**: Access to construction financing
- **Network**: Established relationships with contractors, suppliers, and buyers
- **Marketing**: Professional sales and marketing infrastructure

### 4. Plan for Infrastructure Costs

Infrastructure development — roads, drainage, electricity, water supply, and sewage — can account for 20-30% of total project costs. Plan your budget carefully and include contingencies for unexpected challenges.

### 5. Think Long Term

Land development is a medium to long-term investment. While some projects can yield returns within 2-3 years, the most profitable developments are those planned with a 5-10 year horizon.
