## A New Landmark in Jolshiri Abashon

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of **AHS Jolshiri Central 16**, our flagship residential development located in the premium Sector 16 of Jolshiri Abashon. This marks a significant milestone in our commitment to delivering world-class living spaces in Bangladesh.

### About the Development

AHS Jolshiri Central 16 is a G+2 residential complex featuring 7 meticulously designed apartments. Each unit has been crafted with attention to detail, combining modern aesthetics with functional living spaces.

**Key Highlights:**
- Prime location in Sector 16, Jolshiri Abashon
- 2, 3, and 4-bedroom configurations available
- Rooftop panorama infinity deck
- Direct lakeside parkway access
- High-speed fibre connectivity
- Private security & smart entry access
