## Join Us at the Premier Real Estate Event of the Year

We are excited to announce that AHS Properties & Development Ltd. will be a platinum sponsor at the **Bangladesh Real Estate Expo 2026**, the country's largest gathering of real estate professionals, developers, and investors.

### Event Details

- **Date**: July 15-18, 2026
- **Venue**: International Convention City Bashundhara (ICCB), Dhaka
- **Booth**: Hall 3, Booth A-12 (Platinum Zone)
- **Entry**: Free for registered attendees

### What to Expect

Our team will be showcasing our latest projects, including:

- AHS Jolshiri Central 16 — Premium apartments in Sector 16
- AHS VIP Square — Executive residences in Nayapaltan
- Emara Garden Residences — European-inspired luxury living
- AHS Coastal View Residences — Townhouses with panoramic views

Expo attendees will receive exclusive benefits:

- Waived registration fees on select properties

- Complimentary consultation with our senior advisors
