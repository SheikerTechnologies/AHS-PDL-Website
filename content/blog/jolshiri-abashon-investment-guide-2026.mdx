## Why Jolshiri Abashon?

Jolshiri Abashon is not just another housing project — it is Bangladesh's first planned smart city. Located in the outskirts of Dhaka, this RAJUK-approved township spans thousands of acres and is designed to offer a modern, sustainable, and secure living environment.

### RAJUK Approval — A Mark of Trust

One of the most critical factors when investing in real estate in Bangladesh is ensuring the project has proper government approval. Jolshiri Abashon is fully endorsed by RAJUK (Rajdhani Unnayan Kartripakkha), which means your investment is legally protected. All plots and properties within the development are backed by clear land titles and registered deeds.

### Infrastructure That Impresses

The developers have invested heavily in world-class infrastructure:

- **Wide Roads**: 60-100 ft wide roads with proper drainage systems
- **Underground Utilities**: Electricity, gas, and water lines are all underground
- **Green Spaces**: Multiple parks, lakes, and green belts throughout the township
- **Community Facilities**: Schools, mosques, hospitals, and shopping centres are integrated into the master plan
